## The Genealogy Detective — Your Companion for Family History Discovery
Uncover your family’s story with The Genealogy Detective, a friendly and intuitive companion designed to help you explore your roots with ease and curiosity. Whether you're just beginning your journey or already deep into your research, this app guides you through the clues, connections, and hidden details that bring your ancestry to life.

What The Genealogy Detective offers
• 	Smart organization of your family tree
Build, expand, and refine your tree with a clean, modern interface that keeps everything clear and accessible.
• 	Powerful tools for genealogical research
Track relationships, dates, locations, and historical context — all structured to support both beginners and experienced researchers.
• 	A playful, character‑driven experience

The Genealogy Detective isn’t just a tool — she’s your guide. With charm and personality, she helps you follow leads, uncover patterns, and stay motivated.

• 	Designed for clarity and creativity
From thoughtful visual design to intuitive workflows, every detail supports a smooth and enjoyable research experience.
Discover your past — one clue at a time
